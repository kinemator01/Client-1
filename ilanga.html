Who are we?

Founded in 2024 by Nicholas Phali, Ilanga Landscaping is a growing landscaping company passionate about turning outdoor spaces into beautiful, livable gardens. From small residential yards to larger properties, we offer design, construction, and maintenance services tailored to your vision. We believe every garden should be a natural escape — a place to relax, express yourself, and add value to your home. We focus on affordability, reliable service, and thoughtful design without cutting corners. Whether you need cleanups, hedge trimming, or full transformations, we’re here to help — and we already have a few happy clients to prove it.
